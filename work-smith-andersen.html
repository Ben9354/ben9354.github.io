Junior Electrical Designer

Smith + Andersen

Sep 2025 – May 2026

About

I designed engineering drawings, including single line diagrams and power and lighting layouts using AutoCAD, Revit, and Visual Lighting.

This role heavily involved applying code-based design thinking. This included estimating electrical demand, determining spare capacity constraints, and sizing equipment such as feeders, circuit breakers, and panels to BCBC and CEC requirements.

I wrote electrical planning reports for the electrification of existing equipment and the implementation of EV charging. This work included attending site visits to determine electrical capacity, loading constraints, and potential power savings from heat pump replacements and EV charging.

Lastly, I supported the construction administration phases of multiple projects by writing detailed site instructions and contemplated change notices.

Notable projects I worked on include:

  • Nordstrom Base Building Renovation
  • BMO Van Main
  • FDU @ Oakridge Mall

Key Contributions

  • Designed engineering drawings, including SLDs, power layouts, and lighting layouts using AutoCAD MEP, Revit, and Visual Lighting.
  • Performed electrical calculations for feeders, breakers, equipment, and panels according to BCBC and CEC.
  • Wrote EPRs to electrify equipment and implement EV charging for existing strata buildings.
